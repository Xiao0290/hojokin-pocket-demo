## Recommendation Rule

Experts are scored with a 10-point rule:

- 4.0 points: focus on the selected subsidy round
- 2.0 points: overlap with extracted company keywords
- 1.5 points: consultation mode and service area
- 1.5 points: official-site source quality
- 1.0 point: compliance caveat completeness

Only experts scoring 7.0 or higher are shown in Phase 1. The recommendation is a
candidate list; it is not an endorsement, guarantee, or referral contract.
